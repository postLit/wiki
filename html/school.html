The school badge is a badge on postLit that signifies to everyone that you go to the same school as @rgantzos. This badge comes with the perk of accessing a private chat on the postLit website, which can be accessed by everyone who has it.

Additional Information

Icon

The official icon displayed on profiles and the explore page.

Requirements

All you need to do to get this badge on your profile is provide proof (usually your school email) that you do indeed go to the same school.
