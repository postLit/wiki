postLit Fire

Intrested in getting postLit fire? Look out for special postLit links on the offical Discord server. Read more...

postLit fire is a feature that was introduced to postLit on December 25th, 2022. It allows people who have the badge to upload images to their posts and upload custom banners that show up on your profile! It currently can be obtained by clicking on a postLit fire link that will give you the badge.

Additional Information

Icon

The official icon displayed on postLit fire users profiles and the explore page.

You can easily recognize a postLit fire user because there is a badge on their profiles indicating that they're a fire user.

Requirements

There are not requirements for postLit fire, and as long as you have a generated link that has not been taken by another user, you will be able to get the badge and the perks that come with it!
